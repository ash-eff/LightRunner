Name: The Sun
Diameter: 864,340 mi. / 1.4 million km
Rotation: 24 Earth Days
Temperature: 7,800F to 27,000,000F / 5,500C to 15,000,000C
Interesting Fact: In 5 billion years the Sun will start to become a Red Giant and expand, consuming Mercury, Venus and possibly Earth in the process. After this, the star will collapse down to roughly the size of the Earth, but still contain it's original mass and become a white dwarf.
